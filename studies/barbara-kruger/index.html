Barbara Kruger

Examples of Barbara Kruger's Work

Barbara Kruger uses white text in red boxes as a way to communicate her message. This text is laid over an old black and white photograph that she has found, which is often distorted or filtered to make the image more appealing to look at. The subject of her work is often to do with identity and mental health.

My Work in Response

To replicate Kruger's work, I found black and white photographs on the internet to use in conjunction with the text. The text is generally encouraging and inspiring, however a couple are darker to replicate the cruel realities that Kruger exposes in some of her own work. I used photoshop to create the final images.
