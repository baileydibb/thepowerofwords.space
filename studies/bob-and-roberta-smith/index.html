Bob and Roberta Smith

Examples of Bob and Roberta Smith's Work

Bob and Roberta Smith does lots of colourful but simple artwork focussing in the benefits of art, encouraging a viewer to appreciate art. It usually consists of text on a plain background with strong contrasting colours.

My Work In Response

I began this response by jotting down encouraging phrases about art and planning different layouts before opening photoshop. Then I would use a planned phrase with a layout before choosing colours - I chose saturated, deep colours to mimic the contrast found in Smith's work.
