Examples of Lawrence Weiner's Work

My Work In Response

I thought of things such as odd instructions and directions, as well as adjectives. Then I experimented on Photoshop with different placements and patterns until I found one I liked. I used Blender to place some of my favourites on the wall, similar to what Weiner does.
