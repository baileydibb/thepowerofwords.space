Douglas Coupland

Examples of Douglas Coupland's Work

Douglas Coupland's work consists of simple black text on a single coloured background. The text mostly reveals concerns with modern society, especially ones brought about from the advances in technology. The ultimate goal of his works is to criticise today's obsession with computers. Some phrases are more cryptic and philosophical, leaving them up for interpretaion.

My Work In Response

I thought of and wrote down problems and issues that exist today because of technology, along with some more dark and comedic phrases to use in the work. In Photoshop, I chose a modern and sharp font and selected a background colour at random.
